About Me

My name is Thalal Rafi, and I'm an enthusiastic individual with a passion for creativity and technology. I attended Wycherley International School, where I developed a strong foundation in both academics and personal interests. Growing up, the immersive world of video games always captivated me, not just as a player but also as someone fascinated by the storytelling, strategy, and creativity involved in game design. This curiosity has driven me to explore the intersections of gaming and technology, inspiring me to dabble in coding and digital art in my spare time. When I'm not gaming, I enjoy brainstorming innovative ideas for projects, from designing unique game concepts to exploring the latest trends in technology. My journey is one of continuous learning and creativity, always aiming to blend my passions into something meaningful.
